@messages.at("help.start_session")

Step 1:

You can start a lesson session if the session start date time is within 30 minutes compared to your current local date time.

You should go lesson session page and choose the certain lesson session you want to start. Press the red ‘Start’ button to start.

The center square is your main video stream, which will be showed to your students/users.

Step 2:

Hovering your mouse on the middle square, the top control bar would be appeared:

  • Press ‘mute’ button to mute your voice
  • Press ‘expand’ button to expand full screen.
  • Press ‘sharing screen’ button to share your screen:

For sharing your screen, you must install the EkooEdu plugin.

  • For Chrome user, install it by using this link https://chrome.google.com/webstore/search/ekooedu
  • For rest of browsers, currently not support screen sharing features.
  • You can choose sharing your whole screen or sharing some opened application.
}
